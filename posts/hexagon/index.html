Hexagon Step-by-step strategy

18 Oct 2016 SnailDog
game javascript

Built with HTML5 and JavaScript, this territorial conquest game challenges you to outmaneuver your opponent and claim more ground. Choose between player-versus-player or battle against the AI. The game runs until no valid moves remain.

How It Works

  • Place towers on unoccupied hexes or enemy territory to expand your control
  • Block strategically — you can claim open spaces but cannot overwrite existing enemy towers
  • Review gameplay — view and download the complete game history to analyze your strategy
